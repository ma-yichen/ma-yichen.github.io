## Course Overview

Topics covered:

- Preliminary data analysis and process improvement planning
- Control charts for continuous and discrete quality characteristics
- Statistical process capability analysis
- Product specifications and tolerance analysis
- CUSUM and EWMA control charts
- Design and analysis of factorial experiments
- Acceptance sampling plan design and implementation
