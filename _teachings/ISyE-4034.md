## Course Overview

Topics covered:

1. Data Analytics
   - Regression and Classification, Logistic regression, Generalized
     linear model, Nonlinear regression, Tree-based methods, Support
     Vector Machine, Clustering, Dimension Reduction, Variable Selection, etc.

2. Decision
   - Multi-objective optimization, nonlinear programming, decision in uncertain environment, game-theoretic models, etc.
