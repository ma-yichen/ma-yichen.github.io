## Course Overview

Topics covered:

1. Advanced Continuous Time Markov Chains

- Open Jackson Networks
- Closed Jackson Networks
- Other Markovian Queueing Networks

2. Markov Decision Processes

- Finite Horizon Models
- Infinite Horizon Discounted Models
- Long-run Average Reward Models

3. Sequential Decision Making Under Uncertainty: Some Applications

- Multi-period Inventory Systems
- Production Systems
- Telecommunication Systems
